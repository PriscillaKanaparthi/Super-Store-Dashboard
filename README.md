# 📊 Sales Analytics Dashboard – Power BI

## Overview

This project is an interactive Sales Analytics Dashboard built in Power BI as part of my learning journey during my internship at DataMites.

The dashboard was designed to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights, allowing users to monitor sales performance, profitability, customer behaviour, and regional trends through clear and engaging visualisations.

The objective was to develop practical Business Intelligence skills while gaining hands-on experience in data modelling, visualisation design, and dashboard development.

---

## Project Objectives

* Analyse overall sales performance.
* Track profit and revenue trends.
* Identify top-performing product categories.
* Evaluate sales across different regions.
* Understand customer segment behaviour.
* Present key business metrics in a visually appealing dashboard.
* Support data-driven decision-making through interactive reporting.

---

## Dashboard Features

### 📈 Sales Performance Analysis

Provides an overview of total sales and key performance indicators to monitor business growth.

### 💰 Profit Analysis

Tracks profitability across different categories and regions to identify areas of strong and weak performance.

### 🛍 Product Category Insights

Highlights the best-performing product categories and their contribution to overall sales.

### 🌍 Regional Analysis

Compares sales performance across regions, helping identify geographical trends and opportunities.

### 👥 Customer Segment Analysis

Examines how different customer segments contribute to revenue and profit.

### 📅 Time-Based Trends

Visualises sales performance over time, allowing users to identify seasonal patterns and growth trends.
